Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about medical certification and global practice

PLAB is for practicing medicine in the UK, while USMLE is for the United States. PLAB consists of two parts (written and clinical), while USMLE has three steps. Both require different preparation strategies and we provide specialized training for each.

The timeline varies by country and certification. PLAB typically takes 6-12 months, USMLE can take 12-18 months, and additional licensing requirements may add 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines based on your specific pathway.

While we cannot guarantee placement, our 85% success rate speaks to our comprehensive support system. We provide application guidance, interview preparation, and direct connections with fellowship programs through our extensive network.

Most countries require IELTS scores of 7.0-8.0 overall with specific band requirements. Some accept OET (Occupational English Test) as an alternative. We provide specialized medical English training to help you achieve required scores.

Each country has specific licensing requirements. However, some certifications like USMLE or PLAB can facilitate easier transitions to other countries. We provide guidance on multi-country career pathways and reciprocal recognition agreements.

We provide comprehensive visa and immigration support including document preparation, application guidance, and connections with immigration specialists. Our team helps with work permits, skilled worker visas, and other immigration pathways.
