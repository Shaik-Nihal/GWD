Interactive Training Modules

Structured, immersive, and flexible learning—advance at your own pace, with expert guidance.

Module 1: Foundational Medical Sciences Review

Start Strong

Comprehensive review with engaging video lectures, interactive quizzes, and case-based discussions.

  • Core medical science concepts
  • Integrated clinical application
  • Pathophysiology of common diseases
  • Pharmacological principles
Duration: 6 weeks

Module 2: Clinical Knowledge & Diagnostics

Deep Dive

Master clinical reasoning, diagnosis, and evidence-based management—plus hands-on labs.

  • System-wise clinical knowledge
  • Differential diagnosis skills
  • Lab & imaging interpretation
  • Case-based learning
Duration: 8 weeks

Module 3: OSCE Practical Skills & Communication

Simulate

Live OSCE workshops, simulated patient encounters, and instant feedback for real exam readiness.

  • Simulated OSCE stations
  • History & physical examination
  • Communication skills
  • Ethics & professionalism
Duration: 4 weeks

Module 4: Exam Strategies & Mock Tests

Excel

Realistic mock exams, time management coaching, and stress-resilience workshops.

  • Test-taking techniques
  • Time management
  • Full-length mocks
  • Performance analysis
Duration: 4 weeks
