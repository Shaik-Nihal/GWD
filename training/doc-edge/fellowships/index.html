Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pay after securing a fellowship?
Yes! Our "Success-First" option allows you to pay part of your fee once you receive a confirmed offer.
Do you offer group discounts?
Yes, for two or more applicants from the same institution or group, special rates apply.
Are scholarships available?
Absolutely! We offer scholarships for women in medicine, rural doctors, and merit-based awards.
What's included in the fee?
All mentorship, training modules, one-on-one guidance, and alumni access are included. Visa and licensing fees are extra.
