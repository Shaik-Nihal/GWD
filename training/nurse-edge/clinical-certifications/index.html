Comprehensive Training Modules

Structured learning path covering all aspects of OSCE preparation

Module 1: Clinical Assessment Fundamentals

Master the foundational skills required for clinical assessment including patient history taking, physical examination techniques, and clinical reasoning.

  • Systematic patient assessment
  • Physical examination techniques
  • Clinical reasoning and decision making
  • Documentation standards
Duration: 4 weeks

Module 2: Medication Management & Safety

Comprehensive training in safe medication administration, dosage calculations, and adverse reaction management.

  • Medication administration protocols
  • Dosage calculations and conversions
  • Drug interactions and contraindications
  • Patient education on medications
Duration: 3 weeks

Module 3: Emergency & Critical Care

Essential emergency response skills including CPR, first aid, and critical care procedures for OSCE scenarios.

  • Basic and advanced life support
  • Emergency assessment protocols
  • Critical care interventions
  • Team-based emergency response
Duration: 3 weeks
